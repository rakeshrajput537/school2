About Us

R.C.Central School was established in the year -2018 in the lush green environment at Khagaul , Patna. The school is recognized for its commitment towards excellence. This is an English medium school. At R.C.Central School, students receive so much more than an ordinary education. We pride ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally. Students and families are also welcomed into a dynamic and supportive community.

Admissions 

R. C. Central  School is welcoming interested applications for coming sessions, admission start from Nursery to Class- V . It is subject to vacancies, admission is granted on academic grounds irrespective of caste, creed and community. Students are selected through the results of the admission test that they have to undergo upon registration with the school. Fees are to be paid in "quarterly basis" Admission is based on the performance of the candidate at the written test they are placed in the merit list and admitted as per existing vacancy in a particular class. The candidate is graded as per her performance and suitability for particular standard.
